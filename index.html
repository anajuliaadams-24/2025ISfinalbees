Why bees are worth obsessing over

Bees are tiny logistics geniuses: they move pollen between flowers, helping plants reproduce. When bee colonies struggle, it can ripple into gardens, farms, and ecosystems.

A mini ‚Äúbee hobby‚Äù checklist

  1. Plant bee-friendly flowers
    • Native wildflowers
    • Lavender, borage, and coneflower
    • Herbs that bloom (thyme, oregano, basil)
  2. Avoid pesticides (especially during bloom)
    • Use targeted, minimal treatments if necessary
    • Water early/late to reduce stress on pollinators
  3. Track what you see
    • How many bees per minute?
    • Which flowers get the most visits?
    • What changes month-to-month?
